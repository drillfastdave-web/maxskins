Section 1 – Basics / General Play

  • Stroke Play vs Match Play: Lowest score wins the hole. Penalties add strokes.
  • Ball In Play: A stroke with intent counts. Accidental contact usually does not.
  • Wrong Ball: Stop, correct, +2 strokes.
  • Lost Ball: 3-minute search. Stroke & distance.

Section 2 – Teeing Area

  • Tee within markers, up to two club-lengths back.
  • Ball falling off tee: No penalty.
  • Whiff counts as a stroke.
  • Provisional must be declared.
