PUTTS LOGIC (LOCKED)

Tap cycle: 0 – White flag 1 – Gold flag 2 – Green flag 3 – Red flag 4–5
– Dark red Next tap resets to white.

PENALTY LOGIC (LOCKED)

• Tap = +1 penalty. • Red intensity increases first 1–3 taps. • After 3,

advisory modal opens. • AI explains only. Player must manually adjust
score.

NOTES

SUBMIT FLOW

NOT delete notes. • Does NOT delete prior hole data.
