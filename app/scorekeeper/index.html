  ----------------------------
  CORE UI STRUCTURE (LOCKED)
  ----------------------------

Eight identical vertical pill buttons (2 rows of 4):

TOP ROW (Gameplay Stats) 1. Fairway 2. Sand 3. Green 4. Putts

BOTTOM ROW (Support / Utility) 5. Penalty (Lost Ball / OB – adds
strokes) 6. Notes (Microphone icon – full screen note entry) 7. Fairway
AI Assistant 8. Green AI Assistant

  -----------------------------
  PILL DESIGN SYSTEM (LOCKED)
  -----------------------------

• All pills identical in size and shape. • Black base body with flat
gold ring border. • When activated: - Ring turns solid flat gold. -
Subtle outer glow (premium look). - Light haptic vibration feedback
(tiny “brrt”).

• Images NEVER change between states. • Only overlays change (ball
appearance, glow, flag color, etc.).

  ------------------------
  VISUAL LOGIC – TOP ROW
  ------------------------

FAIRWAY • Base image: realistic fairway, wide cut, no flag. • On
activation: - Small white golf ball appears (~75% toward top). - Gold
ring activates. - Subtle vibration.

SAND • Realistic bunker image (locked design). • On activation: - Small
white golf ball appears in sand. - Gold ring activates. - Subtle
vibration.

GREEN • Aerial 30–60 yard perspective. • No hole visible in base state.
• On activation: - Small white golf ball appears centered. - Gold ring
activates. - Subtle vibration.

PUTTS • Flagstick centered vertically. • Small hole visible. • No ball

putt) Green flag (2 putts) Red flag (3 putts) Dark red (4–5 putts max) •
Max logical putts: 5 • After 5 → reset to white • Number displayed on
flag. • Subtle vibration each press.
